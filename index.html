Our Services

Momentum is a non-profit organisation dedicated to transforming the mental health and well-being of the community.

We offer services such as cooking, gardening, yoga sessions, for the local community across Dalby, Warwick, Toowoomba, Kingaroy, and Chinchilla.

With strategic partnerships such as those with Mindware Psychology, we also provide free psychological therapy sessions.
